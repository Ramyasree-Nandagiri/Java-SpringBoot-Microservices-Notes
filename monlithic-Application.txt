MONOLITHIC APPLICATION
-------------------------------


No Interopabilty it becomes Monolithic application.

multiple functionalities +UI + tightly coupling

DISTRIBUTED APPLICAITON
-----------------------------------

Microservices can be developed with SOAP or REST.
